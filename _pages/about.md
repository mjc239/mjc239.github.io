I'm a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at [PhysicsX](https://www.physicsx.ai/), based in London, where I work on deploying ML models and engineering surrogates to customer production environments.

Previously, I was a Data Science Manager at [C3 AI](https://c3.ai/), developing data science solutions for customers across a range of industries. Before that, I was a Data Scientist and Quantitative Researcher in the Fixed Income division at [Nomura](https://www.nomura.com/).

I completed my PhD in Mathematics at Queen Mary University of London in 2018 — my [thesis](https://mjc239.github.io/assets/PhDThesis.pdf) was on characterisations of black hole spacetimes in general relativity. Prior to that, I studied Mathematics at the University of Cambridge.
